# TWIN Co-Founder Search

## Roles Needed

### 1. CTO / Technical Co-Founder (Equity: 20-30%)
**Responsibilities:**
- Lead AI/voice technology
- Architecture decisions
- Team building

**Requirements:**
- 5+ years in AI/ML or voice tech
- Experience with ElevenLabs, Whisper, or similar
- Full-stack capabilities (Python + React)
- Based in or willing to relocate to Singapore

### 2. CPO / Product Co-Founder (Equity: 15-20%)
**Responsibilities:**
- User experience design
- Feature prioritization
- Customer discovery

**Requirements:**
- 3+ years product management
- B2B SaaS experience
- Multilingual (speaks 2+ languages)
- Network in business/professional circles

### 3. BD / Sales Lead (Equity: 10-15%, salary after funding)
**Responsibilities:**
- First 100 customers
- Enterprise partnerships
- Investor relations

**Requirements:**
- Track record in B2B sales
- Network in target industries (consulting, legal, tech)
- Based in Singapore or major business hub

## Where to Find Them

- AngelList: angel.co
- Y Combinator Co-Founder Matching
- LinkedIn (cold outreach)
- Local startup meetups (Singapore: Block71, Founders)
- Twitter/X tech community

## Interview Process

1. **30-min call** - Vision alignment
2. **Technical/task** - Build small feature or strategy doc
3. **Working session** - 4-hour collaboration
4. **Reference checks**
5. **Offer**

## Onboarding Checklist

- [ ] Sign co-founder agreement
- [ ] Vesting schedule (4-year, 1-year cliff)
- [ ] IP assignment
- [ ] Access to all tools (GitHub, Figma, etc.)
- [ ] First week: shadow all customer calls
